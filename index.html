Hi. I'm Abbas Anees.

Currently I'm a Researcher At OIP Lab, Chungbuk National University, Korea. I hold a bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering from BUITEMS which I obtained in 2014.Throughout my career, I have worked at several leading companies in the telecommunications industry, including TurnoTeck Pakistan and LCC Pakistan. I have also gained valuable experience through internships at Huawei and Alcatel Lucent, where I worked in the RF Department.Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. in South Korea at Chungbuk National University. I have published 17 papers as an author/ co-author in different journals and conferences. I have a particular interest in antenna design, including UWB antennas, wearable antennas, and mmWave antennas.
